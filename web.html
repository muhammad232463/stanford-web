Stanford

A Societal Mission

Stanford University, founded in 1885, is one of the world's leading research universities. Located in Stanford, California, it is known for its academic excellence and vibrant campus life.Stanford was founded almost 150 years ago on a bedrock of societal purpose. Our mission is to contribute to the world by educating students for lives of leadership and contribution with integrity; advancing fundamental knowledge and cultivating creativity; leading in pioneering research for effective clinical therapies; and accelerating solutions and amplifying their impact. More About Academics

Academics

Stanford offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various disciplines, including Engineering, Humanities, Sciences, and Medicine. The university fosters innovation and interdisciplinary collaboration.

While Stanford is celebrated for its achievements in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM), its impact in the social sciences and humanities should not be overlooked.

Catalyzing Discovery in Every Field. Stanford scholars are driven by curiosity to explore the boundaries of knowledge about ourselves and the world, creating the foundation for transformative advances.

Admissions

Admissions at Stanford are highly competitive. The university evaluates students based on their academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and potential to contribute to its diverse community.
